Fried Rice Recipe

Description

Cheap, quick, and easy to make.

Best recipe for stressed university students.

Ingredients

  • Sunflower oil
  • Rice
  • Eggs
  • Shrimp
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut shrimp
  2. Season beat eggs + rice
  3. Make scrambled eggs
  4. Take eggs out
  5. Fry rice
  6. Put shrimp in
  7. Put eggs in
